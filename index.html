WHY I’M HERE

I moved to Nashville at 18 chasing music and quickly realized I was probably the least talented person in the city. That was humbling. It also made me work harder, listen more, and stop pretending I knew everything.

I’ve been writing since I was 12, spent the last decade in and around the industry, and now I know what I want: to be in service of making fucking great music. I’m good in rooms, I care about the process, and I do whatever needs to be done to help artists feel comfortable enough to make their best work.

I don’t think I’ve arrived. I just know I’m committed — to learning smarter, working hard, and doing this for the rest of my life.
